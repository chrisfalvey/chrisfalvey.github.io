Passions and Interests

I have always been interested in technology. In particular, how humans interact with technology to solve problems. As a result of this, and subscribing to the Kevin Kelly view of the Technium, I believe that we as have and continue to live in symbiosis with the tools that's we've created.

This said, I also remain healthily conflicted between the push of technology and desire for minimalism and simplicity.
